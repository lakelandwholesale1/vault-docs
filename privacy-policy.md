# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: July 16, 2026**

We operate the **[The Vault, e.g., The Vault]** mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App, as well as the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the App's tracking and event companion features. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

### A. Personal Data (Voluntarily Provided)
While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you:
*   **Account Information:** Email address, username, and password (if you choose to create an account).
*   **Collectible Inventory Data:** Information you log about your collectibles, including item names, purchase prices, estimated values, categories, and condition status.

### B. Device Permissions and Media
To provide scanning and logging features, the App may request access to certain features on your mobile device:
*   **Camera Access:** Used to scan barcodes via Google ML Kit or to take photos of your items to log their physical condition.
*   **Photo Library/Storage Access:** Used to upload existing photos of collectibles to your portfolio.
*   **Push Notifications:** Used to send you optional reminders for saved events, ticket sales, or status updates.

*Note: You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time through your device's operating system settings.*

### C. Automatically Collected Data
We may also collect information that your mobile device sends whenever you use our App:
*   **Device and Usage Data:** IP address, device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and generic usage statistics (such as time spent in the app and features accessed).
*   **Crash & Diagnostic Data:** Anonymized logs detailing when and why the App crashes, which we use to fix bugs.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
We use the collected data for various operational purposes:
*   To provide, maintain, and support the App.
*   To store your collection catalog and calculate your overall portfolio net worth.
*   To notify you about updates, new features, or local pop-culture events.
*   To monitor usage patterns and improve overall app functionality.
*   To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security vulnerabilities.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services
We respect your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal details or collection logs. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:
*   **Service Providers:** We may use third-party companies (such as cloud hosting or crash-analytics services like Firebase) to facilitate our App. These third parties only have access to the specific data needed to perform these tasks on our behalf.
*   **Affiliate Disclaimer:** The App contains affiliate links to third-party marketplaces and ticketing platforms. If you click on these links and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission. We do not share your private portfolio or personal database records with these third-party merchants.
*   **Legal Compliance:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
