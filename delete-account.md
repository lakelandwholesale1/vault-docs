# The Vault - Account & Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and remove all tracked data from our database, please fill out our deletion request form:

[Submit a Data Deletion Request](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfPY7n7TS-fd90NDSmtj5yuRQcYo1Cqz1LczALfb55jXbEw2w/viewform?usp=sharing&ouid=117321245113008609510)

Once submitted, our team will process your request and delete all your user profile, portfolio, and transaction history within 7 business days.
